# Hafzan Quant Labs

> Independent quantitative research organisation building institutional-grade tools and frameworks across financial markets, corporate finance, and algorithmic trading.

---

## About

Hafzan Quant Labs is dedicated to developing high-precision quantitative tools for traders, analysts, and institutions. Our work spans technical analysis, algorithmic trading, artificial intelligence, and corporate finance — built with a focus on accuracy, performance, and real-world applicability.

We believe in bridging the gap between institutional-level methodology and accessible, well-engineered software.

---

## What We Build

### 📊 Trading Indicators
High-precision price action and market structure indicators for TradingView, built in Pine Script v5. Focused on institutional methodology, multi-timeframe analysis, and order flow.

### 🤖 Algorithmic Trading
Automated trading systems and Expert Advisors (EAs) for MetaTrader 5 (MT5), covering equities, forex, commodities, indices, and crypto markets.

### 🧠 AI & Prediction Models
Machine learning models, AI agents, and quantitative prediction frameworks applied to financial markets. Pattern recognition, regime detection, and probabilistic forecasting.

### ⚙️ Quant Tools & Bots
Standalone quantitative tools, screening engines, backtesting frameworks, and trading bots built for systematic and discretionary traders alike.

### 🏦 Corporate Finance
Analytical frameworks and models for valuation, risk assessment, financial modelling, and capital market analysis.
